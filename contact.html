Consulting Services


I've worked with academics, non-profit organizations, start-ups, consulting firms and international corporations to help them analyze and visualize a wide variety of data. I create both static and interactive visualizations for use in print and online, and can advise you on the best formats to match your goals. I can help make standard charts look beauitful as well as create completely custom visualizations based on your unique data.

My services include the end-to-end data visualization process. I can collect, clean, and analyze the data as well as create the print- or production-ready data visualization. I can also work with you and your team and focus only on the steps necessary to create the visualization design — for example, editing existing figures to make them clearer and more visually engaging or translating a series of static charts into an interactive piece. Please get in touch to discuss what works best for you!

I also enjoy sharing my passion for data visualization with others. I've taught a highly rated short course on data visualization since 2015 and have led several webinars and workshops for different organizations. Please let me know if you are interested in learning more about my teaching services.
